Overview

Types of primitives that a sf::VertexArray can render

Points and lines have no area, therefore their thickness will always be 1 pixel, regardless the current transform and view.

  • Points
  • Lines
  • LinesStrip
  • Triangles
  • TrianglesStrip
  • TrianglesFan
  • Quads
